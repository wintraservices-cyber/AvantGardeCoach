I — The Standing Questions

What brings people here.

01

Doubting the decision

You've turned the same choice over a hundred times and still don't trust your own read on it. Coaching gives that doubt somewhere to go besides in circles.

02

The conversation you're avoiding

Balancing what you value against what's expected of you, while a difficult conversation keeps getting pushed to next week. We work through it before it happens, not after.

03

Losing your own thread

You were a dreamer once. Somewhere between deadlines and other people's expectations, the thread got dropped. We help you pick it back up — without rewriting who you are.

A new role, a new chapter, a new identity you didn't fully choose. The transformation already happened — coaching is where you catch up to it.

III — The Method

Co-active, not prescriptive.

"You have the power to choose when coaching happens, and the strength to choose what you do with it."

— THE AVANT-GARDE PHILOSOPHY

A.

You set the pace

Sessions happen on your schedule, by phone or video — not on a rigid weekly script. Coaching fits around your life, not the reverse.

B.

No shame, guilt, or fear

The work is to understand the impact of your words and choices — explored, never judged. You leave sessions with clarity, not a verdict.

C.

Trained, not improvised

Grounded in Co-Active coaching methodology, with backgrounds spanning leadership training, career counseling, and team advocacy.

D.

Tested in public, not just in sessions

Eighteen published books and a weekly TV talk show — the same ideas you'll work through in coaching, already stress-tested in front of an audience that doesn't let her get away with vague advice. See the books →
